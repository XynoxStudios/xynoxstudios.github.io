WHO WE ARE

XYNOX Studios is a gaming and modding project focused on building unique Minecraft experiences. What started as a solo project has grown into a small but passionate community centered around custom gameplay, server development, and creative experimentation.

Through our Minecraft server and modding projects, we aim to create new ways to play, explore, and interact with the game. By combining custom systems, community feedback, and continuous updates, XYNOX continues to evolve into a growing hub for players who want something different from the standard Minecraft experience.
